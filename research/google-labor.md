# Google Labor Practices Research

## Two-Tier Workforce
- ~50% of Google's workers are TVCs (temps, vendors, contractors) — lower pay, different badges, fewer benefits
- NLRB joint-employer rulings (2023) beginning to challenge Google's legal firewall

## Layoffs
- 12,000 cut Jan 2023 — accounts disabled without warning, while posting record profits ($307B revenue FY2023)
- Ongoing rounds through 2024-25 across hardware, engineering, ad sales

## Union Organizing
- AWU/CWA Local 9009 founded Jan 2021 (~1,200 members)
- Major contractor union wins: YouTube Music Union (41-0 vote, Apr 2023), Accenture/Bard workers (Nov 2023), Google Fiber (Mar 2022), DeepMind UK (2025, 300 workers)
- Google ran anti-union campaign "Project Vivian" and hired IRI Consultants

## Retaliation
- "Thanksgiving Four" fired Nov 2019 — NLRB found illegal termination/intimidation
- Walkout organizers demoted
- Ariel Koren given 17-day relocation ultimatum for opposing Project Nimbus
- ~50 workers fired for Project Nimbus sit-ins (Apr 2024)

## Worker Protests
- Project Nimbus ($1.2B Israeli military contract)
- Timnit Gebru & Margaret Mitchell fired from AI Ethics team (2020-21)
- Project Maven (Pentagon drone AI)
- 20,000-person walkout (2018) over sexual harassment

## Pay Equity
- $118M gender discrimination settlement (Jun 2022, 15,500 women)
- DOL found systemic pay disparities
- Racial discrimination accounts from multiple Black employees

## Global South Workers
- AI trainers/content moderators earning $1.32-$2/hr in Kenya via subcontractors
- Exposed to traumatic content with no mental health support
- Data center workers denied water breaks

## NYC-Specific
- April 2024 sit-ins at NYC offices (9 arrested, workers fired)
- Google's $2.1B+ NYC real estate expansion raising gentrification concerns
- Thousands of TVCs in NYC offices
