These are Freedom of Information Law (FOIL) requests to be filed with NYC agencies to obtain contract details, spending data, and decision-making records related to Google and Big Tech.

## Filing Instructions
- File via each agency's FOIL portal or by email
- NYC agencies must acknowledge within 5 business days
- Must provide records or denial within ~20 business days (extensions possible)
- If denied, appeal within 30 days to the agency's appeals officer
- NYC OpenRecords portal: https://a860-openrecords.nyc.gov/
